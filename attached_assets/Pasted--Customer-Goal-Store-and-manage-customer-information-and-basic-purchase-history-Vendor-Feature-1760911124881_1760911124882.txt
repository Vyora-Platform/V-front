
Customer 
Goal: Store and manage customer information and basic purchase history.
Vendor Features:
•	Add/Edit/Delete customer profiles
•	Personal info: name, contact, email, address
•	Membership/service info: subscription, packages, or lab tests
•	Product info: track past purchases, order history
•	Status: active / inactive / pending follow-up
Admin Features:
•	View all customers across vendors
•	Filter by status, services package, product purchase, or service type all by category
•	Upload customer list (CSV / PDF)
•	No export option will be availabe

Goal: Manage customer bookings and product orders in one place.
Vendor Features:
•	Schedule service appointments or classes for customers
•	Assign bookings to employees/staff
•	Manage product orders: add new order, track order status, return/refund management
•	View full history per customer: services booked + products purchased
•	Notifications to customers (SMS/email) for appointments, deliveries, or order status
Admin Features:
•	View all customer bookings and product orders across vendors
•	Track service completion and product delivery status
•	Generate reports: total bookings, total product sales, repeat customers

Goal: Boost customer retention through service & product engagement.
Vendor Features:
•	Track service usage and product purchase frequency
•	Send reminders for subscriptions, renewals, or product replenishment
•	Create coupon codes or special offers for services and products
•	Track coupon redemption and offer usage
Admin Features:
•	Monitor engagement metrics across vendors
•	Analyze most popular products and services
•	Track retention trends, repeat purchase rates, and customer lifetime value

Vendor Features:
•	Customer dashboard:
o	Visits, service usage, and top products purchased, guarenty worrenty 
o	Revenue per customer (services + products)
o	Task assignment per customer (follow-ups, reminders, product delivery checks)
Admin Features:
•	Marketplace-wide customer analytics:
o	Service vs product revenue comparison
o	Top-performing products and services
o	Customer segmentation by product preferences, service usage, or location
o	Repeat purchase and retention analysis
