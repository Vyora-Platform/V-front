Vendor Features
•	Create New Quotation
o	Customer selection (from customer list or add new manually this customer will automatically gets added into customer module)
o	Auto-fill details (phone, email, address)
o	Select products/services from predefined list or add new custom line items
o	Autofill quantity, rate, taxes , and add additional discount, additional charges for service or delivery charges depending on the product or services(multiple charges addition option)
o	Add optional notes or terms & conditions
o	Auto-calculate total (subtotal + tax + discount)
•	Save, Edit, or Delete quotations
•	Quotation statuses: Draft, Sent, Accepted, Rejected, Expired
•	Unique quotation number & date auto-generated
•	Print / Download / Share as PDF
•	Send via WhatsApp, Email, or link

•	Export all quotations in CSV or PDF
